What does it do?

  • It's time for your next adventure! You just got a new phone, and you're ready to explore all over the world. However, before you go on that voyage of discovery, it would be useful if you could take some precautions to avoid any misconceptions.

    There are many different types of languages around the world (just like there are many different cuisines!), so which one is right for you? No need to worry--Tigrinya Dictionary app can help translate between English, German, Spanish, French, Arabic and Amharic effortlessly...for free!

    To make things even easier when travelling abroad or talking with locals where traditional written language isn't always prevalent about their local cuisine or customs

  • Introducing the Tigrinya dictionary app! We're a global community striving to break down barriers and promote speech, understanding and cross-cultural connections.

    Download this app on your phone right now to learn how to speak! Simply select a language of choice from the leading panel, then touch 'open' for an instant word translation. The best part? NO DATA CONNECTION REQUIRED TO USE THE APP (YAY)!

How to translate words?

  • 1: Open Tigrinya Dictionary App.

    2: Choose two languages.

    3: You can also type the word you want to translate. Just tap the text box, then type a word or phrase and select the word from the dropwon.

    4: You can also switch between the two languages by tapping the icon between the languages.

    5: The word definition with examples will display below the search text box.

    6: You can also save a translation to the Favourites tab for quick access when you need it. Just tap Favourites under the translation to save it.

Main features

  1. You can translate words between Amharic, English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Arabic and Tigrinya.
  2. It has audio pronunciation of the word you are looking up.
  3. Offline access with no internet connection required to use this app! You can still translate without an internet connection or data plan.
  4. Get a word of the day notification on your phone or tablet every morning. This way you'll always know what's happening around the world!
  5. Autocomplete helps you type faster. So that You can find what you're looking for more easily.
  6. Display recently searched words for quick access to them again without having to search through all of them manually.
  7. Your favourite words list will also be displayed.
