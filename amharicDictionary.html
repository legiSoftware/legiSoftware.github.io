Amharic Dictionary

What does it do?

  • What is the Amharic Dictionary app? It's an indispensable tool for learning or translating anything in one of Africa’s most widely spoken and important languages. Add some words to your vocabulary with our all-in-one Amharic dictionary app.

    We love this new kid on the block, because it does way more than just define words! You can use it offline so there’s no need to have data service if you're traveling through Ethiopia, or somewhere where Wi-Fi may be limited.

    Perfect! Remember that foreign word you learned? Use the pronunciation feature to perfect your accent so when meeting people they'll proceed to think how intelligent (and cool) you are. Or check out which words others have.

    Learn a new language on the go with this interactive app that functions both online and offline. It not only has English, Spanish, German, French and Arabic translations of Amharic words but also remembers your favorite terms and sends you word of the day notifications.

    The app also lets you view recent or favourite words in addition to providing audio pronunciations for all terms.

How to translate words?

  • 1: Open Amharic DictIonary App.

    2: Choose two languages.

    3: You can also type the word you want to translate. Just tap the text box, then type a word or phrase and select the word from the dropwon.

    4: You can also switch between the two languages by tapping the icon between the languages.

    5: The word definition with examples will display below the search text box.

    6: You can also save a translation to the Favourites tab for quick access when you need it. Just tap Favourites under the translation to save it.

Main features

  1. You can translate words between Amharic, English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Arabic and Tigrinya.
  2. It has audio pronunciation of the word you are looking up.
