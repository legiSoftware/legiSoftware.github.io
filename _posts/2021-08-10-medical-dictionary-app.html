We have developed the first of its type Multi language medical dictionary app that Defines and translates medical words between 10 languages. With this app medical professionals will be able to communicate quickly with non-English or other languages speakers. Most of the medical terms are derived from Latin, with this app you will be able to translate from Latin to 10 languages as well.

This medical dictionary app is aimed at the medical professionals who works with non-English speakers and general users who who like to defined medical terms in their native languages. This will help medical professionals from spending more time waiting for online translators when they perform their duty.Users will also be able to have good understanding of the medical terms in their Native languages. For example for non-English Tigrinya speakers with the help of this app medical terms will be defined and translated in to Tigrinya.

With almost about 5000 medical words, in this medical dictionary app words translated and written by Dr Kiros Fre Woldu to research and learn about medical terminology, pharmaceutical drugs, healthcare equipment, health conditions, medical devices, medical abbreviations and more, in 10 languages.

You can browse the medical glossary or search medical terms in English, Latin, German, Swedish, Norwegian, Dutch, Hebrew, Arabic, Amharic, Tigrinya and translate the terms to any of these languages.

Other features:

  • Define and translate medical words between 10 languages.
  • Audio pronunciation.
  • Available Offline and Online.
  • UI language setting.
  • Word of the day notification.
  • Autocomplete functionality.
  • Recently searched words
  • Favourite words.
